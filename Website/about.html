About us

Who are we?

We are an non-profit organization that ensures simple phones are accessible to those who want to unplug and refocus on what most matters!

What we stand for?

We draw the line on how dependent we become of technology. We understand technology enhances our lives. Smart phones will become the new cigarette pandemic and we are creating movement to end this smart phone, dumb people pandemic.
